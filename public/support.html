Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app and go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

How do I sign in?

Tap Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google on the login screen. Your account follows you across devices.

How do I turn notifications on or off?

Manage push notifications in the app’s Settings, or in the iOS Settings → Notifications → Gamify screen.

How do I join a game?

Use the share code from whoever created the game and enter it on the Join screen.
