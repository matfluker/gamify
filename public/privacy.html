Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Gamify (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a learning game operated by Fluker Development. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We collect only what the app needs to work. We do not sell your data, and we do not use any third‑party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Information we collect

DataWhy we collect it
NameShown on leaderboards and in the games you join.
Email addressIdentifies your account and lets you sign in across devices.
Apple / Google user identifierAuthenticates you when you use Sign in with Apple or Google. We store the stable identifier the provider returns, not your provider password.
Phone number (optional)Only if you choose to link a phone number to merge an existing account.
Game activity (scores, quiz/test/learn progress, streaks)Powers gameplay, leaderboards, and your progress history.
Push notification token (APNs device token)Lets us deliver the push notifications you opt into (e.g. the daily quiz).

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or advertising identifiers (IDFA).

How we use your information

  • To create and maintain your account and sign you in.
  • To run the games you create or join, including leaderboards and progress tracking.
  • To send push notifications you have enabled. You can turn these off at any time in the app’s Settings or in iOS Settings.
  • To keep the service secure and working correctly.

Sign in with Apple & Google

When you sign in with Apple or Google, the provider sends us a stable user identifier and, on your first sign‑in only, your name and email. We store these to identify your account on future sign‑ins. If you use Apple’s “Hide My Email” feature, we receive and store the relay address Apple provides instead of your real email.

How your data is stored and shared

Your data is stored in our database (hosted on Supabase) and served through our backend (hosted on Vercel). Push notifications are delivered through Apple Push Notification service. We share data with these infrastructure providers only as needed to operate the app. We do not sell or rent your personal data, and we do not share it with advertisers.

Sign‑in requests identify your account using your user ID sent from the app. This is disclosed here for transparency; no advertising or tracking identifiers are involved.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account in the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your user record and all associated data — game memberships, scores, progress history, and your push token — from our database. You may also request deletion by emailing us at the address below.

Children’s privacy

Gamify is rated 4+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
